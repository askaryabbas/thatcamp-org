Storify Embed

Easily embed Storify streams into your blog

Download Version 0.1

  • Description
  • Installation
  • Changelog
  • Stats
  • Support
  • Developers

With this plugin, you can post the URLs of Storify stream, just in the same way you would with YouTube videos or Flickr photos. Put the URL of a Storify page on a new line in your post, and it will be replaced with the stream from storify.com.

This is a simple alternative to the more full-featured Storify plugin, and simply provides embedding on WordPress network sites that don't support pasting of script or iframe tags.

Requires: 2.0.0 or higher
Compatible up to: 3.3.2
Last Updated: 2012-5-17
